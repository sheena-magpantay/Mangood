VITAMIN A

   Mango has a high vitamin A content. A single cup of mango contains around 25% of the daily recommended intake of vitamin A. This vitamin serves numerous vital roles in the body, particularly in the eyes and skin. It also strengthens your immune system, reproductive system, and bones.

VITAMIN C

   Mango is one of the richest food sources of vitamin C. The immune system in your body depends on this vitamin. It also contributes to the formation of bones, muscles, and tendons. Because mangoes are high in vitamin C, eating them enhances plant iron absorption. 46 milligrams of vitamin C, or almost 76% of the daily recommended amount, may be found in one cup of mango.
