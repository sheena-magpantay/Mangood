MANGO SHAKE

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 ripe mangoes
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of condensed milk
  • 8 cubes of ice

STEPS

  1. Rinse and chop the mangoes into dice.
  2. Add mangoes to your blender.
  3. Add 2 tbsp of condensed milk as a sweeter (optional). You may use stevia.
  4. Add 1 cup of milk.
  5. Add the cubes of ice and start to blend.
  6. Blend until the shake is smooth.
  7. Taste test and adjust the ingredients according to your taste.
  8. Pour the mango shake to serving glasses and enjoy!

MANGO GRAHAM

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 cups heavy cream (chilled overnight)
  • ½ cup condensed milk (chilled overnight)
  • Pinch of salt
  • 4 ripe mangoes, chopped or sliced
  • 2 packs graham crackers

STEPS

  1. Peel the skin and slice mangoes thinly. Set aside.
  2. Whip heavy cream until soft peaks form. Then gradually add pinch of salt and condensed milk.
  3. Crush graham crackers until desired crumb or use the whole cracker without crushing.
  4. Let's start layering the mango float in a 8 x 8 inch glass container or a large container. First lay the graham crackers. Then, whipped cream. Lastly, add sliced mango. Repeat one more time. For the last layer, add whipped cream and sliced mango. You can also add more graham cracker crumbs at the top for decoration.
  5. Cover the container and refrigerate overnight for soft texture. Another option is to freeze overnight for ice cream texture.
  6. Serve and enjoy!

MANGO KANI SALAD

INGREDIENTS

  • Kewpie Mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp pickled relish (optional)
  • Salt and sugar to taste
  • 2 pcs iceberg lettuce, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 3 pcs ripe mangoes, diced
  • 12 pcs Japanese crabsticks, shredded
  • 1 pc cucumber, cut into strips
  • 1 pc nori, cut into strips

STEPS

  1. Combine all the ingredients except for the nori in a bowl.
  2. Drizzle the kewpie mayonnaise on top and toss to combine.
  3. Put nori strips to your salad as toppings. Serve immediately.

MANGO PIE

INGREDIENTS

  • Pastry for a double-crust 9-inch pie
  • 4 cups peeled and sliced ripe mango
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon butter, cut into small chunks

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a 9-inch pie pan with half of the pie pastry. Set aside.
  2. Combine mango, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and cinnamon in a bowl; stir until the sugar has dissolved. Transfer mango mixture into the prepared pie pan. Dot the mixture with the butter, then cover with the top crust. Pinch the edges of the top and bottom crusts together to seal. Cut several vents on the top crust.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden brown and the juices are bubbling, about 45 minutes.
